Common questions

How do I unlock new shape categories?
Earn gems every ten points, then spend them to unlock new polyhedra categories.
My progress disappeared — what happened?
Your save is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, or restored your device without a backup, progress may be lost. Unfortunately we don't currently have cloud save. Sorry about that!
The game is too hard / too easy
You can change the difficulty in the settings menu. You can choose between Chill Normal and Intense difficulties.
How do rewarded ads work?
You can optionally watch a short ad to earn bonus gems These are always opt-in — we'll never force an ad on you mid-game. You can also turn off personalised ads in your device settings.
The app crashed or has a bug
We're sorry! Please email us with your device model and version and we'll look into it as fast as we can.
How do I turn off haptics or sound?
Both can be toggled in the in-game settings menu.

The short version

Our game is casual and fun — and we keep your privacy the same way. We don't collect your name, email, or any information that identifies you personally. We just collect some anonymous data to understand how people play, and we show ads to keep the game free.

Analytics

We use Unity Analytics to collect gameplay data. This includes things like which levels are played, session length, and how far players get — but it is non-identifying. We cannot and do not link this data to any individual person.

This helps us fix bugs, balance difficulty, and make the game better for everyone.

You can learn more at unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Advertising

We use Unity LevelPlay to show ads in the game. LevelPlay is Unity's ad mediation platform. It may collect certain device information (such as your device's advertising ID) to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalised Ads.

For more information on how Unity handles data through LevelPlay, see unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy.

Children's privacy

Our game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data retention & sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to improve the game. Ad data is handled by IronSource under their own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the game after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
